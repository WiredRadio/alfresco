Goldsmiths

Beestings

A Monday afternoon musical phenomenon playing the very best of Noise and Glitch and experimental electronic music from the hours of 4PM until 5. Don’t expect any one show to be similar musically, but expect to be equally entertained during each wild ride the Bee will take you on. Listen to their archive of shows here.

Goldcase

A “Live Lounge” style show that takes place every Tuesday morning from 10AM until 11 and showcases the raw musical talent of students here at Goldsmiths, University of London. After hearing the musicians’ perform live on air, we find out a bit more about them personally and what lies behind their original content.
Listen to their archive of shows here.

Brainstorm

An hour’s worth of procrastination every Thursday from 1PM until 2 with your affable and eccentric host Roo. No topic is safe from scrutiny, nothing is too weird to escape discussion on this show – anything goes. Imagine Dr having a radio show basically. Listen to their archive of shows here.
