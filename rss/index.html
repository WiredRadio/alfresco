]]>http://lab.wiredradio.co.uk//contact-us/a7550a4d-7392-4dfa-b0aa-8866af927eb1Fri, 24 Jan 2014 19:05:22 GMT<![CDATA[About Us]]>

Wired Radio is the student radio station of Goldsmiths, University of London, broadcasting online seven days a week and home to over 96 hourly shows every term.

The station is run entirely for and by students, and has existed in various forms and locations since the mid 1990's.

Having been fully revived in the 2012, this year has seen Wired move home, into a brand new purpose built studio in the upper floor of the Students' Union on Dixon Road. We've kept our lovely old flowery sofa, too.

The programming content of Wired is entirely decided upon by our hosts - we don't believe in forcing our shows to use prescribed station playlists and encourage all shows to be as creative and daring as possible (and legally viable).

Our aim as a station is to be as open to the diverse Goldsmiths student base as possible. We allow students to broadcast their favourite music, their thoughts and own work. From hip-hop to Brazilian pop music, Wired is a diverse student media platform.
